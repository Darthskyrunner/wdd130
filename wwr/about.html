History

Riverside Rapids began with Sam Rivers, a local who grew up exploring the wild Thunder River. From an early age, he fell in love with the thrill of paddling rapids and the serenity of the surrounding wilderness. Sharing his passion for the river with friends became his joy, as he guided them through secret trails and taught them how to navigate the water. In 1992, Sam turned his love for nature into Riverside Rapids, a white-water rafting company. His goal was simple: to offer authentic rafting trips that connect people to the beauty of the river and the thrill of adventure. Every guide is a local expert, and each trip showcases not just the rapids, but the wildlife, landscapes, and stories of the region. Today, Riverside Rapids remains a beloved destination, helping adventurers of all levels experience the magic of the river firsthand.
